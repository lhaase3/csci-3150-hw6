My Favorite Movies

This page highlights two movies I really enjoy. I wanted to practice basic HTML text markup and CSS styling while also writing about movies I like. Both films are very different, but each one stands out for its memorable characters and strong overall tone.

The Martian

I really enjoy watching The Martian. One of my favorite parts is when Mark Watney uses science and humor to survive. My favorite scene is his first message back to NASA, and my favorite character is Mark Watney. The cinematography and music make the whole story feel even bigger.

Learn more here: The Martian

Why I like it

  • It mixes science, survival, and humor.
  • The main character stays entertaining even in difficult situations.
  • The visuals make Mars feel huge and realistic.

Office Space

Office Space is a hilarious look at the everyday frustrations of corporate life. My favorite parts are the deadpan humor and the way the characters find small wins in a dull office routine. It is funny, but it also feels relatable because it exaggerates things many people dislike about office culture.

Learn more here: Office Space

What stands out

  1. The humor is simple and memorable.
  2. The characters feel realistic in an exaggerated way.
  3. It makes boring office routines funny.

Final Thoughts

Even though these movies are very different, I like both because they are memorable and easy to rewatch. The Martian is more inspiring and dramatic, while Office Space is more comedic and satirical.
